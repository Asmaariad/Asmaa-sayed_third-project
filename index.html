Popular destination in Palestine

The Dome of the Rock Mosque is located in the Islamic Quarter in the Old City, and it is part of the Holy Mosque. The rock refers to the place from which the Prophet Muhammad, may God bless him and grant him peace, ascended to the highest heaven.

The Kidron Valley separates the Mount of Olives from the city of Jerusalem. Jesus crossed this valley many times, including on Holy Thursday evening when he went with his disciples to the Garden of Gethsemane.

The Church of All Nations is located at the foot of the Mount of Olives. The first church was built in the Byzantine period in 379 AD, over the site where Jesus Christ prayed the last prayer and suffered, so the place became holy. The current church is considered one of the most beautiful churches in the city of Jerusalem, It was built between 1919 and 1924, and was called the Church of All Nations due to the contribution of sixteen European countries to its construction.
